# Number of Counties by COVID-19 Case Count Categories

The map shows that areas with a high number of confirmed COVID-19 cases are mainly concentrated along the border, with the largest numbers around California and New York. The situation in the central United States is relatively better.

The chart on the left side of this map represents the number of counties in each range of confirmed cases within the displayed area. You can zoom in and out to select the area you want to view. You can also click on specific circles to see the county's name and the number of confirmed cases.
